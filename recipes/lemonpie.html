Lemon Pie

Description

The lemon pie is a sublime creation that captures the essence of brightness and zest in every velvety bite. Its delicate, buttery crust serves as a tender canvas for the vibrant symphony of flavors that unfolds with each forkful. The pie's sunshine-yellow hue is not only visually appealing but also a promise of the refreshing burst of citrus that follows.

Ingredients

  • Crust:
    • 1 1/4 cups (150g) all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 2-3 tablespoons ice water
  • Filling:
    • 3-4 large lemons (for about 1/2 cup of lemon juice and 2 tablespoons of lemon zest)
    • 1 1/4 cups (250g) granulated sugar
    • 1/4 cup (30g) cornstarch
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1/2 cup (120ml) heavy cream
    • 1/4 cup (55g) unsalted butter
  • Topping(Optional)
    • Powdered sugar, for dusting
    • Whipped cream

    • Steps

    1. Crust:
      1. In a food processor, pulse the flour and salt to combine.
      2. Add the cold butter cubes and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
      3. Gradually add ice water, one tablespoon at a time, and pulse until the dough starts to come together.
      4. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface, shape it into a disc, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
      5. Roll out the chilled dough on a floured surface and fit it into a 9-inch pie pan. Trim the excess and crimp the edges. Chill again while preparing the filling.
    2. Filling:
      1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
      2. Zest and juice the lemons. Set aside.
      3. In a saucepan, whisk together the sugar, cornstarch, and salt.
      4. In a separate bowl, lightly beat the eggs and then whisk in the lemon juice and zest.
      5. Gradually whisk the lemon mixture into the sugar mixture.
      6. Place the saucepan over medium heat and add the heavy cream and butter.
      7. Cook the mixture, whisking constantly, until it thickens and boils.
      8. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for a couple of minutes until it's very thick.
      9. Remove from heat and pour the filling into the prepared pie crust.
    3. Baking and Serving:
      1. Bake the pie in the preheated oven for about 15-20 minutes, or until the filling is set and the edges of the crust are golden.
      2. Let the pie cool completely on a wire rack before serving.
      3. Optional: Dust with powdered sugar and serve with whipped cream.

Enjoy your delicious homemade lemon pie!

Note: This recipe is a basic guideline. You can adjust the sweetness and tartness of the filling to your preference and also experiment with different crusts or toppings.
